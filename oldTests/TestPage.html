Brick City Cafe

F&A Home » Dining Services Home » Brick City Cafe

The Brick City Café is centrally located in the Al Davis Room of the Student Alumni Union. The Brick City Café seats approximately 500 people and provides a quiet, well-lit dining atmosphere for chatting with friends, studying, or small luncheon meetings with colleagues.

The café, a popular location amongst students, faculty, and staff, offers a variety of premium fresh, healthy meals and features daily a "Five for Five" offering -at least five meals for $5 or less!

News!

RIT was one of the first colleges in the nation to commit to cooking with Zero Trans Fat oil as a healthier option. The frying oil is made from a custom blend of specially formulated oils. Its unique formula, combined with a patented ultra purification process, provides enhanced frying performance.
